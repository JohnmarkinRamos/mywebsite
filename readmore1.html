The Perfect Burger

Choose the Right Meat: Use 80/20 ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat) for the juiciest patties.

Season Well: Salt and pepper are essential! Add spices like paprika or garlic powder for extra flavor.

Forming the Patty: Avoid overworking the meat. Make a small indent in the center to prevent puffing while cooking.

Cooking Tips: Sear on high heat for a perfect crust. Flip once and avoid pressing the patty to keep the juices inside.

Perfect Toppings: Fresh lettuce, tomatoes, crispy bacon, and melted cheese elevate your burger experience.
